Our Story

Pedal Pros isn't just a company—it's a cycling family that's been pedaling through life's adventures since way back in 1937. Picture this: a group of bike enthusiasts gathering in a cozy garage, tinkering away with gears and chains, fueled by nothing but a love for two-wheeled freedom. That's where it all started, and that spirit of camaraderie and passion still runs deep in everything we do.

From those humble beginnings, Pedal Pros has grown into something special. We're not just about selling bikes; we're about sharing the joy of cycling with everyone we meet. Our shop is like a hub of excitement and good vibes, where folks from all walks of life come together to talk bikes, swap stories, and maybe even find their next adventure companion.

And speaking of bikes, we've got a lineup that'll make your heart race faster than a downhill sprint. From sleek roadsters to rugged mountain machines, each Pedal Pros bike is a labor of love, handcrafted with care and attention to detail. We believe that every pedal stroke should feel like magic, and our bikes are designed to make that happen.

But it's not just about the bikes—it's about the people. Our team is a quirky bunch of bike nuts who live and breathe cycling. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a newbie on training wheels, we're here to welcome you with open arms and help you find your perfect ride. Because at Pedal Pros, we're not just selling bikes—we're building a community, one pedal at a time.
